About the game

After the "CONTINUE" button which is located on lower-right corner of the game screen, is activated, click on it. Then write a name that you want to use in game. Click on "CONTINUE" button. Click on "START" button to start to game. Controls of the game: You can control the racket by "MOUSE" You can serve by clicking on "SERVE" button when you or your opponent fail of hitting ball. After you completed a match, you can proceed next match by clicking on "NEXT LEVEL" button. During the match, you can go back to main menu by house icon located on upper-right corner of the game screen. You can mute or unmute the game sound by its icon located on upper-left corner of the game screen. Have fun
